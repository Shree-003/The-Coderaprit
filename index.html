About Expensify

Welcome to Expensify, developed by The Coders - a team of passionate developers dedicated to creating financial solutions that make life easier.

Our application helps you track your income, expenses, and savings in one convenient place. With powerful visualization tools and smart insights, you'll always know where your money is going.

Features:

  • Track income, expenses, and savings separately
  • Multiple currency support
  • Interactive financial dashboard
  • Smart AI assistant for financial questions
  • Secure login and data protection
  • Responsive design for all devices
  • Dark mode for comfortable night-time use

We're constantly improving Expensify to bring you the best personal finance experience. Stay tuned for more features!

Help Center

Getting Started

To begin tracking your finances:

  1. Create an account or login if you already have one
  2. Add your first transaction using the form
  3. View your financial dashboard for insights
  4. Use the chatbot for quick questions

FAQs

How do I change my currency?
Use the currency selector in the top left corner to switch between different currencies.

Can I export my data?
Yes, go to the Reports section and click "Export Data" to download your transactions.

Is my data secure?
Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your financial information.

How do I enable dark mode?
Click the moon icon in the bottom left corner to toggle between light and dark themes.
