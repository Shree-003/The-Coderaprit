About Expensify

Welcome to Expensify, developed by The Coders - a team of passionate developers dedicated to creating financial solutions that make life easier.

Our application helps you track your income, expenses, and savings in one convenient place. With powerful visualization tools and smart insights, you'll always know where your money is going.

Key Features:

  • AI-Powered Insights: Get smart spending predictions
  • Financial Health Score: Track your financial wellness
  • Receipt Scanning: Automatically log expenses
  • Multi-Currency Support: Track finances in any currency
  • Secure Authentication: Full login/register system
  • Dark/Light Mode: Comfortable viewing anytime
  • Expense Categories: Detailed categorization
  • Search Functionality: Quickly find transactions
  • Data Export: Download your financial data

We're constantly improving Expensify to bring you the best personal finance experience!
